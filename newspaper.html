Should you take the Class?

Yes: 90% No: 10%

Why You Should Take this Class...

- This class is super fun if youhave friends in the class. After you finish your stories, you usually have some days of free time where you can just talk or play games with your friends.
- In this class, you learn a lot about the stress and fun things that come with working on a newspaper. You also learn about a lot of skills and techniques that are required to know when working in a real newspaper. These skills include writing different types of stories, creating the newspaper pages, coming up with grarphics, taking photos at events, and uploading online stories.
- This class allows you to have fun while also learning a ton of real-life necessary skills!

Why You Shouldn't Take this Class...

- The stress that comes when the printing deadline comes is a lot to deal with.
- This class can be very hard if you find it difficult to talk to people for interviews or with writing stories. If you don't like talking to other or writing stories, this isn't the class for you because that is what newspaper is all about.
